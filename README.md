# Nemo-Loves-Clicks (NLC)

> *A chaotic, satisfying idle clicker game built for the Hack Club BEEST YSWS Event.*

Nemo Loves Clicks is a pixel art idle clicker game where you torment adorable **Nemojis**, unlock clankers, battle the most gigachad boss, collect memories, and connect to **NemoNet** an inbuilt online community & leaderboard.

---

## Features

### Clicker Gameplay
- Satisfying idle clicker mechanics.
- Combo system.
- Critical hits.
- Damage multipliers.

### Nemojis
- Multiple unlockable Nemojis.
- Ability to equip your fav Nemojis from the Memories window.
- Hidden secret sussy baka Nemoji.
- Memory collection system.

### Clankers
- Buy up to 4 Clankers.
- Automatic clicking companions.
- Upgrade them with Clanker Juice.
- Secret achievement for defeating a Nemoji using all 4 Clankers.

### Shop
Purchase upgrades including:
- Buy up to 10 tiny Cursor that revolve around you basically adding plus one click to every click of yours.
- Clankers (Autoclicker).
- Clanker Juice (To activate and increase Clanker attack speed).
- Energy Drinks (Increases critical hit chance).

### Mail System
Receive in-game mails:
- Welcome mail.
- End game mail.
- Glowing notification system when new mail received.

### Achievements
Unlock numerous achievements while progressing through the game.

### Boss Battle
Face off against the most glorious, my gigachad, my love, my king!!! **Sans** in an intense boss battle. (not so intense ;-;)

### NemoNet
A built in online community and leaderboard powered by Supabase.

Features include:
- Global leaderboard.
- Public message board.
- Saves username.
- Automatic refresh for new messages.
- A thin security system to prohibit people from sending messages greater than 200 characters or HTML tags.

### Audio Settings
Toggle individual sounds including:
- Background Music
- Critical Hits
- Ora Ora SFX (kinda broken right now)
- Clankers
- Achievements
- Cards
- Boss Music
- Shop music

### Save System
Automatic save/load using LocalStorage.
